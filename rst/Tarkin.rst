   {{Wikipedia|Tarkin (codec)}}

Tarkin is an obsolete `lossy <lossy>`__ video compression codec created by `Xiph.org <Xiph.org>`__. Development `halted <http://lists.xiph.org/pipermail/vorbis-dev/2005-August/018213.html>`__ to focus on `Theora <Theora>`__.
