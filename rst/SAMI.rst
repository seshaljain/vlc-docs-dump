The SAMI (*.smi) `subtitle <subtitle>`__ format is a **rare** -format with startframes given for each subtitle.

Example:

::

   <TITLE>Title of film
   <STYLE TYPE="text/css">
                             <blank line>
   <BODY>
                             <blank line>
   <SYNC Start=30886>
   Oh no!  The eggs are hatching!
                             <blank line>
   <SYNC Start=34037>
   Wait, never mind.  I was hallucinating.
