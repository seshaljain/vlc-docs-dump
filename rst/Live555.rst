'''LiveMedia''' from '''Live555''' is a [[library]] for streaming data
over a [[RTSP]], [[RTP]] and [[SDP]] connection.

Syntax:
   vlc <nowiki>rtsp://%5Buser:[passwd]@]serverip:port/options</nowiki>
