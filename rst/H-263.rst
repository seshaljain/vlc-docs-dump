H.263 is a standard for video compression. It is not free and open. It was preceded by `H.262/MPEG-2 Part 2 <H.262/MPEG-2_Part_2>`__ and succeeded by `H.264/MPEG-4 AVC <H.264/MPEG-4_AVC>`__.
